About me

In 2020 I attended the Poznan University of Technology on Mechatronics Engineering. Throughout the studies my interests were developing, from the mechanical engineering to the industrial use of AI.

During my bachelor's studies, I focused mainly on mechanical engineering skills like 3D modeling, finite elements analysis and creating mechanical documentation. I was a part of two student's scientific research groups; where I designed f.e. a robotic arm and other mechanical parts with scanning. My bachelor's thesis focused on creating the sollution for a problem with manual taking the measurements of EMG signals on the forearms. My proposed sollution not only provided the ability to do the measurements autonomously but also allowed EMG signals to be mapped in the space. The thesis was defended with the highest grade of 5, and the sollution is currently undergoing the patent process.

During Master's degree studies I developed my software engineering skills, diving into the C# and Python with machine learning, which led me to create a Master's thesis which covered the tasks of classifying objects using deep learning models and using the Intel RealSense D435 depth camera, integrating this system with the UR3 cobot and creating a digital twin in the MuJoCo engine. On the last semester I joined the GHOST student's research group to deep dive into a Computer Vision, and the practical use of the Artificial Inteligence
