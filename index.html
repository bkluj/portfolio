About me

In 2020 I attended the Poznan University of Technology on Mechatronics Engineering. Throughout the studies my interests were developing, from the mechanical engineering to the industrial use of Artificial Inteligence.

During my bachelor's studies, I focused mainly on mechanical engineering skills like 3D modeling, finite elements analysis and creating mechanical documentation. My bachelor's thesis focused on creating the sollution for a problem with manual taking the measurements of EMG signals on the forearms. My proposed sollution not only provided the ability to do the measurements autonomously but also allowed EMG signals to be mapped in the space. The thesis was defended with the highest grade of 5, and the sollution is currently undergoing the patent process.

Every project we undertake is crafted with attention to detail, ensuring that form follows function while never compromising on aesthetic beauty.
